About Payless Online Travel

Our History


History is an important consideration in your choice of a travel management company. It represents the company's strengths and abilities to succeed in the most challenging of circumstances.

PAYLESS ONLINE TRAVEL is closely affiliated to Flight Express Travel Inc. and has been in operation for 20+ years. With multiple locations in US, Canada, and overseas; we combine the expertise of a corporate big budget agency with the affordability and personal attention of a smaller agency.

PAYELSS ONLINE TRAVEL is a member of the exclusive provider Ensemble Travel group and is independently recognized as being a leading international focussed travel management company in Calgary’s retail travel market . This recognition has proven to be beneficial to the unique travel needs of the leisure and corporate travelers.

Relationships that may benefit your travel arrangement program include:

» Direct relationships with most major Airlines, cruise vendors and tour operators in association with and powered by our affiliate, Flight Expess, (USA ,Canada and overseas)

»Cost saving advantages through world recognized suppliers that include, best rates and special package pricing proven to save money for you, the traveler.

Through the combined attributes of local expertise and global resources, Payless Online Travel is committed to all your travel needs. Our product to you is expert personal service at affordable fares and rates that are often cheaper than most online fares, and our goal is 100% traveler satisfaction.

If you are not convinced yet, then consider all the available specials we offer. If you forward your name, email and type of travel to info@flypayless.ca, we will email you the available specials or you can check out our online tools.

Our experience is your guarantee of satisfaction.
